What is a floor price?

A floor price is the minimum guaranteed value of a Floors token. The protocol backs every token with real reserves — so no matter what the market does, the floor holds and only ever goes up.

How does the floor price rise?

Trading fees, liquidity rebalancing, and staking yield all compound into the protocol's reserves. As reserves grow relative to supply, the floor rises automatically — no action needed from holders.

What does liquidation-free borrowing mean?

You borrow against your floor value with no liquidation risk. No health factors, no margin calls, no expiry dates. Because the floor never falls, your collateral is always safe. Repay on your own schedule.

Why no external liquidity providers?

Every Floors token launches with its own AMM funded by protocol reserves. Buy or sell at any time directly against this pool — no LPs to attract, no liquidity fragmentation, no thin order books.

Who is Floors Finance for?

Issuers who want credible, sustainable tokenomics — and investors who want DeFi exposure without liquidation risk or collapsing liquidity. If you've been burned by tokens that dumped to zero, Floors was built for you.
