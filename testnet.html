Your Points, Your Tier

Testnet points convert directly into your tier assignment. No hidden caps. No late-entry discounts. The points you earn determine the allocation you keep.

Higher Tier, Better Access

Tier 1 wallets (250+ pts) access vault capacity and presale slots before anyone else. Complete all four paths to secure your position at the top.

Fair Play Enforced

Wash trading, sybil wallets, and gaming without genuine usage result in disqualification. The system rewards real testers — not point farmers.

What defines a floor price?

Floors Finance uses Protocol Owned Liquidity (POL) to back tokens. This creates a programmatic guarantee where the treasury acts as the redeemer at the floor price — backed by real reserves, not promises.

Is there liquidation risk?

Zero. Because you borrow against the floor price, and the floor price is backed by hard reserves, your collateral value can never drop below your debt position. No margin calls. No health factors.

What are the tier thresholds?

Tier 1 requires 250+ points and unlocks maximum allocation and first vault access. Tier 2 starts at 200 points. Tier 3 at 150. Wallets below 150 are marked Incomplete — they still earn a base allocation, but Tier 1 earns disproportionately more.

How do points convert to tokens?

Your final points balance determines your tier. Your tier determines your proportional share of the genesis allocation pool. Up to ~300 points are available across four earning paths — complete them all to reach Tier 1 and maximise your allocation.

Has the protocol been audited?

Yes. The Floors Finance protocol has been independently audited. The full report is available under Protocol → Audit in the footer. Security is a first-class concern at every layer of the system.

When does Phase One end?

Phase One is live now. Once the presale closes, the token floor is set permanently. Every participant after the close only pushes it higher — join early for the lowest floor ever.
